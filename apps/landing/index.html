Drag-and-drop runsheets with per-item timers. Overtime alerts, auto-advance, and keyboard shortcuts. Every operator sees the same live state in real time.

Device Control

Control your audio console, video switcher, lights, and streaming software from one interface. Faders, scene recall, tally — like Companion, but cloud-native.

Production Chat

Built-in production comms with message types: standard, alert, and cue. Or connect your existing Slack, Mattermost, Teams, or Discord — ShowPilot becomes the interface.

Lower Thirds

Push names, titles, and scripture to OBS or vMix with one click. Three built-in styles, instant preview, and a transparent overlay that works with any browser source.

Bring Your Own Stack
