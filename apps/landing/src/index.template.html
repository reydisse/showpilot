FAQ

Questions teams ask before switching

Do our volunteers need accounts?

No. Crew check in by scanning a QR code — no account, no app install, no password. Accounts are only needed for operators who run the show (and every plan includes unlimited operators).

Can we keep ProPresenter, OBS, and the rest of our gear?

Yes — that's the point. ShowPilot is an integration layer first: it connects to ProPresenter, OBS, vMix, Blackmagic ATEM, Behringer X32/Wing, OnTime, Slack, Mattermost, Teams, Discord, and more. Where you don't have a tool, ShowPilot's native features fill in.

What do we need to install?

Nothing for most teams — ShowPilot runs in the browser on any laptop or tablet. Controlling local hardware (like ProPresenter on your venue network) uses a small bridge app on one machine; everything else is fully cloud-based.

Our venue internet is bad. Will it work?

ShowPilot is built for 5 Mbps venue Wi-Fi. Operator actions sync to every screen in under 300 ms on a normal connection, and the interface is designed to degrade gracefully — you never lose your rundown mid-show.

What does per-organization pricing mean?

One subscription covers your whole organization: every operator, every volunteer, every device. There is no per-seat math and adding crew never raises your bill.

Is the founding member rate really locked for life?

Yes. Teams that subscribe at the founding rate of ${{founding.monthly}}/mo during the launch window keep that price for as long as the subscription stays active — it includes everything in {{pro.name}}.
